01 — The Challenge
🔥

The Problem

Warehouse operations were stuck in spreadsheets, manual order copies, and missing stock visibility. Small teams needed a simple Android tool instead of expensive enterprise software.

  • Manual Excel tracking — slow and error-prone
  • No unified view of multi-platform e-commerce orders
  • Pick-and-pack errors generating costly refunds weekly
  • No bin location system — staff wasting time locating items
  • Enterprise WMS software priced out of reach
⚡

The Solution

I built Mini WMS as a lean Android warehouse platform — order sync, barcode picking, and subscription billing for small operations.

  • Live dashboard with stock, orders, and bins at a glance
  • One-tap sync from eBay, Amazon & Shopify
  • Barcode scanner for accurate, fast picking
  • Structured bin location system for organised fulfilment
  • Excel import/export — familiar to any operations team
