01 — The Challenge
🌍

The Problem

Farmers had no mobile way to book tractors, milling, or payments. The value chain was largely analogue, slow, and disconnected.

  • No digital access to tractor, harvester, or milling services
  • Payments cash-only — slow, risky, and informal
  • No marketplace to buy or sell rice paddy at fair prices
  • Cooperative societies had no tech-enabled distribution channel
  • Farmers couldn't access Go Africa's services without physical presence
🌱

The Solution

I built the Android marketplace and service-booking app that digitises booking, payments, and product discovery for rural farmers.

Sole Android engineer on a Fiverr-led delivery team, shipping a production-ready app in 6 months.

  • Book tractor, harvester, and milling services from a phone
  • M-PESA and Flutterwave payment — instant, secure, inclusive
  • Marketplace to buy and sell rice paddy and processed grain
  • Google Sign-In for fast, frictionless farmer onboarding
  • Launched live on Google Play for Kenya & East Africa
