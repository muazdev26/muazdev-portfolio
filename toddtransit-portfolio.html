01 — The Challenge
🚚

The Problem

Drivers were stuck on paper, chat, and spreadsheets. The fleet needed a single Android app to capture delivery proof and speed routes.

  • No single app for route details, photos, and proof of delivery
  • Multiple image attachments were cumbersome and slow
  • Signature capture was manual or missed entirely
  • Delivery reports were hard to validate after the shift
  • Internal rollouts demanded secure distribution, not public release
📱

The Solution

I built the delivery capture Android app from scratch — photos, signatures, sync, and internal Play deployment.

Built with a small operations team and shipped for fleet rollout.

  • Multiple delivery photos per stop with on-device compression
  • Digital signature capture and delivery confirmation on the phone
  • Offline-capable form flows with fast sync via Retrofit
  • Internal Play Store distribution for secure driver rollout
  • Driver workflow built for speed and low-bandwidth regions
